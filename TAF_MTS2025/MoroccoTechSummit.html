Morocco Tech Summit
Marrakesh, April 14-16/2025

"Unleashing the Boundaries of Tech..."

The Morocco Tech Summit, also known as GITEX Africa, is scheduled to take place from April 14-16, 2025, in Marrakech, Morocco.
This event will bring together global tech leaders, startups, investors, and government officials to explore and discuss the latest technological advancements and opportunities within the African continent ​
